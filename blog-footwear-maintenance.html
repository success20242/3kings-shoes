Footwear Maintenance Tips for Long-Lasting Shoes

Proper care keeps your shoes looking new and ensures durability:

  • Regularly clean dirt and dust with a soft brush or damp cloth.
  • Use shoe polish or conditioner to maintain material quality.
  • Store shoes away from direct sunlight and moisture.
  • Rotate shoes to avoid excessive wear on a single pair.
